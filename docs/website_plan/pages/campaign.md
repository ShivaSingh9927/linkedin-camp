# Campaigns

The **Campaigns** tab contains three subpages:

1. **Campaign List** — Default landing page  
2. **Message Templates** — Hub for managing outreach copy  
3. **Queue** — Operational dashboard for automation + LinkedIn limits

---

# 1. Campaign List (Default Landing Page)

The main hub where users manage active, paused, drafted, or archived campaigns.

---

## **Layout & Navigation**

- Uses a **standard two-column layout**
- Left sidebar is **fully expanded**
- Under the **Campaigns** accordion:  
  - **Campaigns List** is highlighted in blue with a **solid left border** (active state)
- Top-right area contains the **floating header pill**:
  - Subscribe  
  - Start a campaign  
  - Credits pill  
  - Notification  
  - User Profile

---

## **Main Content Area**

### **Header**
- Large title: **My campaigns**
- To the right: **Start a campaign** (primary button)

---

### **Action Bar**
A pill‑shaped segmented filter control with four tabs:

- **Running** (blue play icon)
- **Paused** (dark pause icon)
- **Draft** (dark pencil icon)
- **Archived** (dark box icon)

Each tab includes a **count badge**.

To the far right:
- **Search bar**

---

### **Center Canvas (Empty State)**

- Illustration: An astronaut reading a pink book floating in zero gravity
- Message:  
  **“You don’t have any campaigns here”**
- CTA Button: **Start a campaign**

---

# 2. Message Templates

A dedicated workspace for managing saved LinkedIn or Email templates.

---

## **Layout & Navigation**

- **Dual-sidebar layout**
- **Primary left sidebar collapses** into icon-only view
- A new **Messages Sidebar** appears with categories:

### **Categories**
- **LINKEDIN**
  - Message  
  - Invitation  
- **EMAIL**
  - Email  

The currently selected item is shown as:
- White card
- Subtle shadow
- Blue highlight

---

## **Main Content Area**

### **Header**
- Large title: **Messages management**
- Sub-header displays the **current template category**
  - e.g., “Message”  
  - Includes: Blue paper plane icon  
  - And: “LinkedIn” (small grey text)

---

### **Center Canvas (Empty State)**

- Illustration: Blue alien standing confidently among space rocks  
- Text: **“No templates”**
- Tooltip icon for more info  
- CTA Button: **Create a template**

---

# 3. Queue

The operational dashboard showing queued actions and daily limits.

---

## **Layout & Navigation**

- **Secondary sidebar disappears**
- Returns to a **two-column layout**
- Primary left sidebar stays in **collapsed icon-only** mode

### **Columns**
- **Left column:** Pending queued actions  
- **Right column:** Daily quotas (LinkedIn safety limits)

---

## **Main Content Area – Left (Queued Actions)**

### **Header**
- Title: **Queue**
- Sub-header:  
  - **Queued actions**
  - Red badge: **Inactive**
  - Button: **Update activity**

---

### **Filters**
Rounded pill-style filter tabs:

- LinkedIn  
- Email  
- My campaigns  
- Action type  

---

### **Center Canvas (Empty State)**

- Illustration: Same blue alien from templates page  
- Message: **“No action for the moment”**
- CTA Button: **Start a campaign**

---

## **Main Content Area – Right (Daily Quotas)**

### **Header**
- Title: **Daily quotas**
- Subtext: *Updates daily*

---

### **LinkedIn Limits**
A vertical list of white cards, each with:

- Metric icon  
- Remaining actions  
- Horizontal progress bar  

### **Tracked Metrics**
- **Invitations** — e.g., 87 left  
- **Messages** — e.g., 136 left  
- **Profile visits** — e.g., 145 left  
- **Follows** — e.g., 91 left
